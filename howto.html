Programme 01

Light Weight
Training

Resistance training is the single most evidence-backed intervention for post-menopausal health. It builds muscle, protects bone density, improves balance, and boosts resting metabolism. We start light. We progress smart. And we celebrate every single lift.

Kelley teaches strength using a hypertrophy-first approach — controlled, effective movements that build lean muscle safely. If you like structure, ask about LTL, our 8‑week strength programme with clear weekly progressions and technique coaching.

◆ Beginner, intermediate, and advanced streams
◆ All equipment provided — dumbbells, bands, TRX
◆ Small groups of max 12 — proper coaching every time
◆ Technique-first coaching every session
◆ Joint-friendly modifications always available

Programme 03

Nutrition 1:1
consultations

Nutrition at The Wellness Hub is private, practical, and calm. These are 1:1 sessions designed for real life after menopause — energy, sleep, protein, digestion, and habits that actually stick.

◆ 1:1 consultation (private)
◆ Protein + bone health focus (without diet culture)
◆ Simple weekly plan built around your schedule
◆ Follow‑up check‑ins available
Enquire / book 1:1 Read the guide

Note: Nutrition consultations are booked separately from class drop‑ins and membership. We’ll confirm availability + pricing when you enquire.

What a consult looks like
◆A quick intake (goals, routine, appetite, sleep, energy)
◆A clear “what to add” plan (protein, fibre, hydration)
◆Simple meal ideas that match your week (no perfection)
◆Optional follow‑up to adjust based on what’s working

Seasonal programmes

Structured courses
when you want them

Not everyone wants random drop-ins. Sometimes you want a clear start date, a plan, and momentum. These programmes run a few times per year.

LTL — 8 weeks
A progressive strength block built around technique, confidence, and consistency. Great if you’re new to lifting or you want structure.
Hyrox — 8 weeks (seasonal)
Endurance + strength blend for members who like a goal and a community challenge. Runs twice per year. Supportive, not intense-for-the-sake-of-it.
Pilates — weekends
Weekend Pilates with Sara Connor (Saturday + Sunday). Calm cueing, strong fundamentals, and progress you can feel.
Mobility — weekly
Gentle mobility sessions to keep joints moving well. Great alongside strength and Pilates.
