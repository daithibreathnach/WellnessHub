The Wellness Hub uses LegitFit for scheduling and payments. That means you always see live availability, your memberships, and your booked sessions in one place.

Quick steps
1) Open the booking link
2) Create an account (or sign in)
3) Buy Drop‑in (€12) or Membership (€80/month · 2 classes/week)
4) Book your sessions
5) Manage cancellations and upcoming sessions in LegitFit
